About FromSoftware

FromSoftware is a Japanese video game developer founded in 1986. They are best known for creating some of the most challenging and atmospheric games in history, including the Dark Souls series, Bloodborne, Sekiro, and Elden Ring.

Their games are famous for deep lore, punishing but fair difficulty, and a unique style of storytelling through item descriptions and environmental details.

Browse the Games page to see their library, or visit the Characters page to learn about iconic bosses and heroes.
